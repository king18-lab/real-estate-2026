Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How can I schedule a site visit?

+

You can schedule a site visit by filling out the contact form, calling our sales team, or visiting our office. We offer both weekday and weekend visits based on your convenience.

Do you offer home loan assistance?

+

Yes, we have partnerships with leading banks and financial institutions. Our team will guide you through the entire loan application process and help you get the best rates.

What documents are required for booking?

+

You'll need identity proof (Aadhar/PAN), address proof, passport-size photographs, and income documents for loan purposes. Our team will provide a complete checklist.

Are there any hidden charges?

+

No, we maintain complete transparency. All costs including registration, GST, maintenance, and other charges are clearly explained in the agreement before booking.

Can NRIs invest in your properties?

+

Absolutely! We welcome NRI investments and have specialized services including virtual tours, remote documentation, and dedicated NRI relationship managers.
